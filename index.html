I received my master's degree in artificial intelligence from National Chiao Tung University, Taiwan, where I was advised by Yu-Chee Tseng and Jen-Jee Chen under PAIR Labs.

My research interest lies in machine perception, including sensors and computer vision, as well as machine learning such as deep neural networks and generative modeling. I am aslo interested in design optimization, which involve search algorithms, design space encoding, and objective functions. My curiosity extends to their applications in cyber-physical systems and human-computer interaction.

I am particularly fascinated by the unsupervised approaches that can infer semantics. My research goal is to develop effective inductive biases that capture useful properties and establish metrics and heuristics that highlight certain qualities. I strive to be an interdisciplinary researcher and am continuously exploring and developing my research interests.
